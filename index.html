Delhi Transit Analysis

Leveraged GTFS feed (bus routes & stops) to analyze and improve Delhi’s public transport network. Parsed spatial and trip data using Python & SQLite, uncovering congestion patterns, low-coverage zones, and trip inefficiencies.

Key Highlights:
